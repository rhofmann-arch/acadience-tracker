Place benchmark print materials here using this naming convention:

  Student materials:  {grade}_{period}.pdf
  Scoring materials:  {grade}_{period}_scoring.pdf

Examples:
  K_MOY.pdf              — student booklet for Kindergarten MOY
  K_MOY_scoring.pdf      — teacher scoring sheets for Kindergarten MOY
  1_BOY.pdf              — student booklet for Grade 1 BOY
  1_BOY_scoring.pdf      — teacher scoring sheets for Grade 1 BOY

Grades: K, 1, 2, 3, 4
Periods: BOY, MOY, EOY
(K has no BOY)

These are served at /acadience-tracker/materials/ and linked from
the Assessment Manager.
